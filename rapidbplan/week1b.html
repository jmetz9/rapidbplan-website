The 'One Page Business Plan'

Let's get to work!

Rapid planning means we jump right in and get started! On this page, work through the following steps:

1. Download and save this Worksheet PDF document to your local computer drive or a USB drive.

2. Print it out or fill it in (AND SAVE!) with the information you already know or assume.

3. IMPORTANT TIP - Start a list of the information you do not have yet to keep track of progress when doing research. (this does not have to be a fancy spreadsheet or typed document - a handwritten list or notebook works best!)
